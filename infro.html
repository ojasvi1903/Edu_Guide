Edu-Guide: Personalized Learning System

Empowering students through data-driven insights and tailored recommendations for academic success and holistic development.

Project Overview

The Edu-Guide project leverages machine learning to analyze various student attributes, including academic performance, study habits, and wellness factors. Its primary goal is to predict a student's performance category and provide highly personalized recommendations for improvement and advanced learning.

System Architecture Flow

Edu-Guide operates through a robust architecture involving offline model training and an interactive online application, all supported by a persistent database.
